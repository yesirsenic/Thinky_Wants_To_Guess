Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the game Thinky Wants To Guess (the "App"), developed and provided by an individual developer ("Developer").

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, for the purpose of improving service quality and providing advertisements, third-party services (such as Unity Ads) may collect non-personal information including advertising identifiers, device information, and usage data.

2. Purpose of Use

  • Providing advertisements and analyzing ad performance
  • Improving app stability and service quality
  • Providing AI-based features (drawing analysis and result generation)

3. Data Storage and Retention

The Developer does not store or retain personal data.

Any data processed through third-party services (advertising SDKs or AI services) is handled according to the policies of those providers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following external services:

  • Unity Ads (for advertisements)
  • OpenAI API (for AI-based drawing analysis and text generation)

5. AI and Data Processing

The App uses an external AI service to analyze player drawings and generate text-based results during gameplay.

Player drawings may be temporarily transmitted to external servers (such as OpenAI API) for processing. The data is used only to generate results and is not stored by the Developer.

The Developer does not collect or store personally identifiable information, and no personal data is used during AI processing.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13, and the Developer does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to changes in laws or services. Any updates will be posted on this page.
